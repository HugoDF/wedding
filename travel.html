Travel

Plane

If you are flying from abroad your best options are Thessaloniki Makedonia Airport (SKG) and Skiathos Airport (JSI). You can find flights to Thessaloniki from most major European cities. Easyjet and Ryanair have direct flights from London, Manchester, Paris (Beauvais), Brussels (Charleroi), and Warsaw (Modlin). Once you arrive in Thessaloniki you can travel to Pelion by bus, car/taxi or train.

If you wish to combine your time in Greece with a visit to beautiful Sporades why not fly directly to Skiathos? Airberlin, Transavia, Thomas Cook, and Monarch airlines might offer convenient flights. Very close to the airport you will find Skiathos’ port. From there you can take the ferry to Volos (duration and price vary). It is a 15 minutes drive from Volos to Portaria (a taxi ride should cost approximately €20, you can pick a taxi from the street or order one at +30 24210 2777).

If you wish to fly to other Greek airports or you are not able to find convenient flights from your city let us know and we will try our best to help with your search.

Car/Taxi

Avis, Hertz and Sixt rental companies operate in various locations in Thessaloniki including the airport. It is a 2.5 hours drive from Thessaloniki to Portaria.

Alternatively, you can car pool with some of our other guests or arrange for a taxi to pick you up from the airport at a cost of €190 one way (each taxi can fit a maximum 4 people). Please let us know via e-mail if you are interested in that option.

Bus/Coach

There are several coaches a day from Thessaloniki to Volos. The coach ride lasts approximately 2.5 hours. For timetables and fares visit Thessaloniki Bus (Coach) Station’s website here.

If you arrive to Thessaloniki by plane, bus No 78 will take you directly from the airport to the coach station.

Train

The train takes between 2 and 3 hours from Thessaloniki to Volos. For timetables and fares from Thessaloniki or other cities visit the TrainOSE website here.

If you arrive to Thessaloniki by plane bus No 78 will take you directly from the airport to the train station.
